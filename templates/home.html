About DeepFake Detection Studio

Fighting Misinformation with AI

DeepFake Detection Studio is an advanced artificial intelligence system designed to identify synthetic or manipulated media content. In an era where deepfake technology is becoming increasingly sophisticated, our mission is to provide reliable tools for media authentication.

Our Technology

We leverage state-of-the-art deep learning architectures including MobileNetV2, combined with sophisticated image processing techniques. Our system analyzes multiple aspects of media files:

  • Facial feature consistency analysis
  • Temporal coherence in video frames
  • Attention heatmap generation
  • Multi-file comparison capabilities

Our Mission

To democratize access to deepfake detection technology and help individuals, organizations, and platforms verify the authenticity of digital media, thereby combating misinformation and protecting digital identity.

Technology Stack

Python TensorFlow Keras OpenCV MTCNN Flask MobileNetV2 Deep Learning
